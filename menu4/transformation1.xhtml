Crown of Polish Mountains

About the Crown

The concept of the Crown of Polish Mountains was presented in 1997 by Marek Więckowski and Wojciech Lewandowski in the June issue of the magazine "Get to know our country". Initially, the list of peaks contained 27 peaks. When announcing the idea of ​​the KGP, it was not only about relieving the most popular mountains, especially the Tatras, and popularizing many other attractive but unknown ranges, but also about in-depth knowledge of these regions of the country, with their history, nature, geological past and contemporary culture.
